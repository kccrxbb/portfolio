Burger Waffle Breadcrumbs

What are those?

Burger menus are the little 3-line icons you see in almost every website nowadays. When you click them,
typically a menu of some sort appears to allow for simpler website navigation.

Waffles are a similar sort of menu, that appear as a 3x3 square icon of dots. Waffles tend to display apps
however and are normally used for cloud services.

Breadcrumbs! Very easy to picture what these are. Breadcrumbs are a sort of 'timeline' of the pages you
visit on a site. They're used to make back and forth-ing esier and in general make page browsing more simple.

Collapsed Sidebar

You can click on the burger menu/bar icon to open the sidebar, and push this content to the right. I think it's quite cool.

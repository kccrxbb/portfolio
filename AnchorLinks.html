Anchor Links

What are anchor links?

The anchor link is a means to going between pages on a website with links in the form of '< a href>' (hypertext reference) and '< /a>'.
Some examples of anchor links include the Content Area links you use to navigate my page and the button you pressed to get here!
These links can be customised and given additional functionality such as 'target="_blank"', this opens the link on a new page.
